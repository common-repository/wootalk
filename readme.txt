== Description ==

For any E commerce Business, client communication remains the most crucial aspect of customer service, after order placement. Having struggled to be responsive to our ever increasing customer base, WooTalk as a solution was just natural.  
Wootalk is the outcome of all the problems that we face while communicating with our clients. WooCommerce just doesn't have any solutions when it comes to being responsive to your loyal customer base. 

Launched as a WooCommerce compatible plugin, WooTalk allows vendors and customers to Send/Receive messages right from their Order dashboard, after the order is placed. You can keep track of all your customer communication through dedicated individual conversation panels in continuation to previous message history. WooTalk is highly user friendly, Simple to setup and easy to use. 

Email, Message Notifications make sure you never miss a message from your customers providing no fail ability to respond. Prepare Dynamic Response templates,  Mark and update Tickets, Get Notified in Real Time, Get on top of all your customer communication. WooTalk is designed to solve all your communication woos.

== Installation ==

=== From within WordPress ===

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
1. Search for 'Wootalk'
1. Activate Wootalk from your Plugins page.
1. Go to "Wootalk admin menu".

=== Manually ===

1. Upload the `wootalk` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the wootalk plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to "after activation" below.
